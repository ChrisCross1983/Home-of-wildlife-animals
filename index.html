About us

Our Story

Follow your passion

Welcome to "Home of Wild Animals," an organization dedicated to the protection and preservation of the fascinating wildlife in Tanzania, particularly in the Arusha region. Our passion for animals and nature inspired us to establish this initiative. We are committed to safeguarding endangered species, conserving their habitats, and raising awareness about the importance of wildlife protection.

With a dedicated team of experts and volunteers, we work tirelessly to find sustainable solutions for wildlife conservation and promote a harmonious coexistence between humans and nature. By collaborating closely with local communities and international organizations, we ensure that our efforts have long-lasting positive impacts and contribute to the preservation of biodiversity in the Arusha region.

Our Mission

Animals

Helping the wild animals

Our mission starts with providing direct assistance to wild animals in need. We support rescue operations, rehabilitate injured animals, and release them back into their natural habitats. Through our programs, we ensure that endangered species are given a second chance.

We collaborate closely with local communities and international organizations to guarantee that our efforts have long-lasting positive impacts and contribute to the preservation of biodiversity in the Arusha region.

Humans

Enabling and involving the people

A crucial part of our work is empowering and involving people. We firmly believe that true conservation can only be achieved through the active participation of the community. Through educational programs, workshops, and volunteer projects, we offer people the opportunity to engage directly in wildlife protection.

By spreading knowledge and awareness, we inspire a new generation of conservationists and wildlife advocates in Tanzania.

Nature

Balance between Animals, Humans and Nature

Our ultimate goal is to create a balance between animals, nature, and humans. We advocate for the sustainable use of natural resources and promote eco-friendly practices that benefit both wildlife and local communities.

Through research projects and collaboration with scientists, we develop innovative approaches to protect wildlife habitats while addressing the needs of the human population. Our holistic approach aims to create a world where animals, nature, and humans can coexist in harmony, ensuring a thriving ecosystem in the Arusha region.
